Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Quantum Energy Beds

QWhat is a Quantum Energy Bed?

The Quantum Energy Bed is an advanced wellness system that combines multiple therapeutic technologies including far infrared heat, vibrational therapy, negative ions, chromotherapy, and magnetic energy to promote relaxation, detoxification, and overall wellness.

QHow long is a typical session?

A typical session lasts between 30-45 minutes. Most users start with 30-minute sessions and can extend as they become more comfortable with the experience.

QIs it safe for everyone?

The Quantum Energy Bed is generally safe for most people. However, we recommend consulting with your healthcare provider if you are pregnant, have a pacemaker, or have any serious medical conditions.

QHow often should I use it?

For optimal results, we recommend 2-3 sessions per week. However, even one session per week can provide significant benefits.

QWhat should I wear?

We recommend wearing comfortable, loose-fitting clothing made from natural fibers like cotton. You'll remain fully clothed during the session.

QWill I sweat?

Yes, the far infrared heat will likely cause you to sweat, which is part of the detoxification process. We recommend drinking plenty of water before and after your session.

QWhat benefits can I expect?

Many clients report improved sleep, reduced pain and inflammation, increased energy, better mood, enhanced mental clarity, and an overall sense of well-being.

QHow quickly can I get my Quantum Energy Bed?

If your chosen model is in stock, you can expect delivery within 7-10 business days. For made-to-order units, the typical lead time is 30-45 days. We'll keep you updated throughout the process and coordinate delivery to ensure a smooth arrival.
